Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 15, 2025

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Identity Data: Your name.
  • Contact Data: Your phone number, email address, or messenger ID.
  • Location Data: Your city and time zone to facilitate communication.
  • Travel Details: Dates, number of guests, preferences, interests, and any other information you provide in the request form.
  • Technical Data: Cookies to ensure the functionality of the website and to collect anonymous analytics.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide consultations and respond to your inquiry.
  • To prepare personalized travel proposals.
  • To organize and book services (hotels, flights, transfers, etc.).
  • To provide support and assistance during your trip.

3. Data Transfer to Third Parties

We transfer your data to third parties (such as hotels, airlines, and local partners) only when it is necessary to fulfill your booking. We ensure that all our partners adhere to high standards of data security.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. The storage period is limited to the time required to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data upon request.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data, as well as the right to restrict its processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact details provided on our website.
